About Us

Ungineering is an idea conceived by two students of NIT Durgapur, who felt there was something missing in the whole duration of their undergraduate lives. It aims to provide a learning platform to the students where they will become capable of building and deploying software products right from scratch. To know how it works, read FAQ.

How it all happened?
It is a badly kept secret that no one talks sense during daytime in an engineering college. Hence, one night under the influence of an alkaline substance and a full moons light, Anuj Kalbalia and Avishek Basu Mallick had an epiphany: that they have learned a lot in college and none of it was in the classroom. (Wish they had attended more lectures to give the classrooms a fair chance) They did not want to be called engineers anymore just by the virtue of having a degree. (A profound realization that only getting a job or an admission in the top B-School can provide) They knew that if they opened their textbooks again, it would still be Hebrew, Greek and Latin. (Assumption here is that they have opened those books before) However, thanks to their involvement in the campus activities (where all the name, fame and sometimes a dame resides), they had a belief that they could take on any full time project and execute it, both as an idea and as a technique.
