What? Why? How? 
Hi,

I will just take a few seconds of your time to tell you what Ungineering is.

Ungineering is a concept which was born out of the experience I had as a student of IT engineering in NIT Durgapur. I realized that I had the potential and the urge but I did not have the platform.

Ungineering is THAT platform. I believe every Computer Science/IT student has the potential to develop a software from scratch which me and you can use on a day to day basis on our personal computer, tablet, phablet or smartphone. All he/she requires is a little bit of proper guidance and the exposure to work on a live product. Ungineering will provide both of these.

If I made sense, please check out the "How" and "Why" sections. For any other queries, please feel free to mail me at anuj@ungineering.com
